Grocery Store

The Gilded Gourd is a charming establishment with a thatched roof and wooden walls adorned with climbing ivy. Inside, the store is illuminated by glowing lanterns hanging from exposed wooden beams. The shelves are stocked with a bountiful assortment of local produce, herbs, and handmade goods, creating a welcoming and earthy atmosphere.
The atmosphere of The Gilded Gourd is bustling yet harmonious, with townsfolk of all sorts coming and going. The air is always filled with the fresh scents of herbs and spices mixed with the earthy aroma of fresh produce. The shopkeeper greets each customer with a warm smile, creating a friendly and communal vibe where locals often pause to exchange news and gossip.

Elara Thistlewood

Elara Thistlewood is a middle-aged woman who hails from a long line of herbalists and grocers. She inherited The Gilded Gourd from her parents and transformed it into one of the most beloved shops in town, known for its quality produce and rare ingredients. Elara is deeply connected to the community, often sponsoring local events and festivals.

Elara is a woman of average height with a warm smile and sparkling green eyes that reflect her friendly nature. Her hair is a tangle of curly auburn, often pulled back with a simple leather band. She dresses in comfortable, earth-toned clothes that allow her to work easily around the shop. Elara is known for her persuasive charm and a keen sense of humour, making her a memorable and liked figure among the townsfolk. Her mannerisms include a thoughtful chin tap when considering a deal and a habit of humming softly while arranging her goods."
